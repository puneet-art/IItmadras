FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the IIT Madras degree a real, recognised degree?

Yes. It is an officially recognised BS degree awarded directly by IIT Madras. It carries the same institutional credibility and is accepted globally.

What exactly is UnsaidTalks' role in this program?

UnsaidTalks provides the offline campus, coaching for the degree, industry curriculum, and placement pipeline.

What does the 2+2 format mean exactly?

2 years of academic foundation + 2 years of industry immersion (paid co-op) while finishing the degree.

Are the internships in Years 3 and 4 paid?

Yes. All industry immersion placements are structured, paid positions at partner companies.

Can I join if I'm from a Commerce background?

Yes. Any stream is welcome. IIT Madras qualifier provides a bridge for non-science students.

What happens if I don't secure an internship?

We offer 100% placement support from Year 1, including bridging workshops and career coaching.

How matches it from doing degree alone?

Doing it alone lacks the ecosystem. FUEL provides mentorship, network, and real-world skills.

Does it replace the IIT Madras program?

No. You are an IIT Madras student. FUEL is the professional support campus built around it.
